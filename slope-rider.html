About Slope Rider

Slope Rider is a top-tier winter-themed endless runner where you sled down infinite icy slopes through snowy forests and frozen caves. Dodge falling trees, rolling snowballs, ice boulders, and collect gift boxes to unlock new sleds — survive longest to smash high scores!

How to Play Slope Rider

Your sled auto-rushes down random snowy tracks each run, with speed ramping up for an adrenaline surge.

Mission: Stay centered, avoid sudden hazards like crashing trees, giant rolling snowballs, ice rocks, roots, wooden fences, collapsing bridges, and deep pits — one hit = instant game over!

Simple controls, extreme challenge:

  • LEFT/RIGHT arrows: Steer to dodge edges.
  • UP arrow or SPACEBAR: Jump over low obstacles, keep momentum.

Collect gift boxes (Christmas presents) along the way to buy new sleds in the shop: faster speed, higher jumps, better handling (snowmobile, metal sled...). Complete daily missions (6 tasks/day like crashing fences on purpose) for 10–100 extra gift boxes. Every run is unique thanks to procedural terrain — snowy forests, ice caves, narrow bridges!
