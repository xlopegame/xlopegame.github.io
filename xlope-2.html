About Xlope 2

Xlope 2 is the addictive sequel to the classic Slope game — a 3D endless runner where you control a neon-green ball racing down an infinite spiraling slope in a vibrant neon world, dodging deadly red obstacles and accelerating wildly to smash global high scores!

One ball. Endless speed. Total thrill

Slope 2 turns you into a neon speed demon, racing a bright green ball down twisting green paths full of traps and jumps. It's simple to start but impossible to master — perfect for anyone who loves quick action and big challenges. Every second tests your focus and speed. Ready to roll? Play now at https://xlopegame.github.io — works on any device, no downloads, super smooth.

THE STARTING BLAST: HOLD ON TIGHT

Your game kicks off with a wild drop — the green ball shoots out of a dark tunnel, dodging red blocks before you even get your bearings.

It hits 50 mph in just seconds.

  • Your first job: Swerve past three surprise red blocks, then hit the first jump.

Mess up? You're right back in, ready for round two — keeps the fun going non-stop.

THE BASIC FLOW: ROLL, JUMP, SURVIVE

Xlope 2 is all about this easy loop that gets harder every time:

  • Roll straight — The ball moves on its own along glowing green roads.
  • Grab a boost — Yellow spots make you go even faster.
  • Launch up — Hit ramps to fly through the air, using arrow keys to steer a bit.
  • Land safe — Stick the landing or crash into a wall.

Smart trick: Link a few boosts together to stay in the air longer and skip big dangers below.

7 BIG TRAPS (AND QUICK WAYS TO BEAT THEM)

Red Blocks: These bright red cubes blow up your ball if you touch them. Fix: Quick side-step right before you hit one.

Tiny Paths: Super narrow turns that make you slip off easy. Fix: Stay right in the middle and make tiny moves only.

Big Gaps: Huge empty spots after jumps that you have to clear. Fix: Aim for the ramp's edge and hold your direction in the air.

Slippery Slopes: Roads that tilt and throw you off balance. Fix: Steer against the slide to stay in control.

Tunnel Walls: Red blocks inside tunnels that end your run fast. Fix: Keep dead center and don't look at the sides.

Moving Walls: Big pillars that slide back and forth. Fix: Wait for the open spot and go through on the way out.

Trick Boosts: Yellow speed spots that lead straight to a red wall. Fix: Slow down hard if you see danger ahead.

COOL EXTRAS YOU CAN GET

As you play, grab shining gems that float along the way:

  • 50 gems: Unlock a basketball look for your ball (plays the same, just looks fun).
  • 100 gems: Get Magnet Mode for 3 seconds — pulls in more gems automatically.
  • 200 gems: Unlock Ghost Ball once — lets you pass through one red block without dying.

Everything is just for style — no buying advantages here.

HOW TO HIT BIG SCORES (FROM ZERO TO 1000+)

  • First 200 meters: Get comfy — learn the jumps and grab two boosts in a row.
  • 200 to 500 meters: Find your groove — guess what's coming and spend more time flying.
  • Over 500 meters: Go pro — ride on top of tunnels to skip most ground traps.

Top score right now: Around 1,800 meters. Beat it and share your win online for instant bragging rights.
